Local Food

Typical culinary from my hometown, Kediri.

Tahu Takwa

Tahu Takwa is a yellow tofu dish, caused by the addition of turmeric to the water in which it is soaked. Furthermore, it has a chewy, fine-pored, and soft texture. In terms of taste, tahu takwa has a savory flavor without any sourness at all. When fried, the outside becomes dry and crispy, while the inside remains soft and chewy.

Nasi Pecel

Pecel is a dish consisting of boiled vegetables, such as long beans, spinach, bean sprouts, which are doused in peanut sauce. The peanut sauce that is poured over pecel is called pecel sauce, which is made from a mixture of galangal, brown sugar, salt, chili, torch ginger, kaffir lime leaves, and roasted peanuts that are mixed, pounded, or ground.

Gethuk Pisang

Getuk pisang, a specialty of Kediri, is made from rajanangka bananas. This snack has a distinctive sweet taste, a soft, chewy texture, and a fragrant aroma from its banana leaf wrapping. It's often taken as a souvenir.
