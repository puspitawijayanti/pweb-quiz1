Tourist Places

A short guide to tourist destinations in my hometown.

Simpang Lima Gumul

Simpang Lima Gumul (SLG) is a monument located in the middle of five road intersections, precisely in Tugurejo Village, Ngasem District.

Dolo Waterfall

Dolo Waterfall is a waterfall located in Besuki, Jugo Village, Mojo District, Kediri Regency, East Java.

Candi Tegowangi

Tegowangi Temple is a temple located in Tegowangi Village, Plemahan District, Kediri Regency, East Java, Indonesia.

Mt. Kelud

Mount Kelud is an active volcano 1,731 meters above sea level in East Java, Indonesia, located on the border of Kediri, Blitar, and Malang.
